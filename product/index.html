Product

Prodigy decides what to build next, turns it into tickets, dispatches agents, and ships the code.

The product has four parts: deciding the next opportunity, turning it into assignable ticket packages, dispatching validated tasks to coding agents, and tracking the work all the way to shipped code. Explore the flow below or reach out on Contact.
